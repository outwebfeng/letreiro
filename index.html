What is Letreiro Online?

Letreiro Online is an innovative web tool that allows you to create eye-catching digital LED signs right from your browser. The term "letreiro" is associated with digital LED displays often used for advertising, conveying messages, and grabbing attention in public spaces. Here at Letreiro Online, we give you the power to design and customize your very own digital marquee messages.

Digital LED signs are a great way to attract attention, whether you are promoting a business, sharing important information, or just having fun with creative messages. Our tool offers a simple and efficient way to design these signs without needing expensive software or complex hardware. Whether you need a scrolling sign for a shop window, an event, or a personal project, Letreiro Online has got you covered.

How to Use Letreiro Online?

Using Letreiro Online is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to create your custom marquee:

  1. Enter your desired text in the input box labeled "Enter your marquee text here."
  2. Select the font color and background color to personalize your sign.
  3. Set the scroll speed to control how fast your message moves across the screen.
  4. Click on "Update Marquee" to see your changes in action.
